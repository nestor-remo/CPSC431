About Thebaus

Simon "Baus" Hofverberg is a League of Legends streamer/esports player, currently top laner for Los Ratones. He also has nearly one million subscribers on both YouTube and Twitch.

Thebausffs is a well-known League of Legends streamer and high-elo player, famous for his unique Sion playstyle. He often plays on the EUW server and is recognized for his inting Sion strategy, where he deliberately dies in fights to maximize tower damage using Sion’s passive.

Accomplishments/Fun Facts

  • Made appearances as a guest caster and analyst in the LEC 2021 Summer.
  • Travelled to Korea with several other streamers and content creators in April 2022, where he reached the highest rank in the server as a Challenger player
  • Appreciated in Europe for his very endearing streaming persona and known for several terms he popularized such as "we shilling", "solo bolo", "wpgg", and more.
  • Beat Faker, the most renowned esports player of all time, in a 1v1 during a professional match.
